About Us

Founded in 1956 by Arnold Young, Young & Champagne Company has built a solid reputation as one of Michigan's leading electrical manufacturers representative firms. Specializing in valued added electrical and lighting products to the residential, commercial, industrial and utility markets throughout the state of Michigan.

Young & Champagne was formed as the Arnold J. Young Company merged with the Louis H. Champagne Company in October of 1962. After the retirement of Lou Champagne in 1982, Arnold J. Young became President of the company. In April of 1988, Michael Young became president and the company was succeeded by its current management team (second generation ownership), where it remains a driving force within the Michigan electrical industry.
